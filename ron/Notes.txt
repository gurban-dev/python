Big O (O): Represents the upper bound or worst-case time
complexity of an algorithm's running time.

Ω (Omega): Represents the lower bound or best-case time
complexity of an algorithm's running time.

Θ (Theta): Represents the tight bound or average-case of
an algorithm's running time.

In computer science, a traversal is the process of visiting
each element inside of a data structure.

In colloquial/everyday English, you could say the following:
I will traverse mount Kilimanjaro in Tanzania.
