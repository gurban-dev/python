The program should print the names of the jobs in the order in which they received service from the computer.

For each job, the program must also print:

Its priority

The processing time it required

The waiting time before it began execution

Example:

For the following input:

N 3 11
E 4 30
T 6 15
A 7 21
F 8 25
H 5 18
The program output should be:

1 F 8 25 0
2 A 7 21 25
3 T 6 15 46
4 H 5 18 61
5 E 4 30 79
6 N 3 11 109
(Assuming no new jobs arrived during the program execution.)
