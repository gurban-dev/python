The NOT bitwise operator (~).

num = 60

128  64  32  16     8    4    2    1
2^7  2^6  2^5  2^4   2^3  2^2  2^1  2^0 
 0    0    1    1     1    1    0    0

60 can be represented in binary:
0011 1100

Applying the NOT bitwise operator:
~num

0011 1100 -> 1100 0011

-(60) - 1 = -61
The formula for the ~ (NOT) bitwise operator:
-num - 1 = -61
